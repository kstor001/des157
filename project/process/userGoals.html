How will your users understand the content of the project?

Users will be prompted with small alerts that inform the user of ways they can interact with the application and what it is capable of doing. When the user arrives at the main page, tht prompts will inform the importance of the app and suggest ways the user can navigate through it.

How will your users understand the primary objectives?

The user will understand the benefits of this app through the use of it's functions. The more a user uses this app, the more beneficial it can be to their lives. The app will also suggest ways that it can help and encourages users to interact as much as possible.

What content and features do your users need, and in what form do they need it?

The users will be provided with an interactive flower petal diagram that charts their emotions in a visually appealing way which encourages the use of the app in order to create more visually appealing flowers. Users need data to be stored and the app will serve as a guide to ease the use of the app.

How do your users think your interactive projects should work and what are their experience levels with the type of media you are creating?

My users typically use applications that have information laid out in charts; they are not used to unusual diagrams to plot data. My users follow the prompts on screen in order to understand how they should interact with the application.
